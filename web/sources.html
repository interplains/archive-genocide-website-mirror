This documentation was collected from 391 journalists, monitors, and channels — many of them Palestinians who filmed from inside Gaza, and some of whom were killed doing so. The archive credits them; it does not replace them.

A representative selection is listed below. The complete, searchable source list is maintained on the project's official site. Every clip in the gallery also links back to its own original post where one exists.
