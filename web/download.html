Keep it alive

The archive survives because people re-host and re-seed it. If you can:

  • Seed the torrent — leave it running in your client after downloading.
  • Run a mirror — this whole kit is open-source; host a copy so the site itself can't be taken down.
Have footage to contribute? Submissions go only through the project's official, verified channel — never through a mirror. This copy has no upload form on purpose, to protect both you and the integrity of the evidence.
