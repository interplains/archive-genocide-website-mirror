Where the footage is from

The archive carries no GPS data, so this counts clips whose captions name each area — a rough picture of where the documentation is concentrated (… located mentions). Click a region to open its footage.

Counts are derived from place names in each clip's caption; a clip may mention more than one area, and roughly half of clips name no location at all. This is an indication of coverage, not a precise incident map.
