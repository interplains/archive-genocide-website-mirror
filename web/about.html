Why it exists

Footage disappears — accounts get suspended, phones are lost, platforms delete. This archive collects that documentation into one deduplicated, classified, and independently verifiable collection so it cannot be quietly erased. Every clip is traced to its source, checked for scope, and the whole release is cryptographically signed.

Why you're seeing this copy

This is a mirror — an independent, self-hosted copy of the archive that anyone can run. That's the whole point: the more copies exist, the harder the evidence is to erase. No single takedown, host, or country can remove it. If you can, run your own mirror and seed the torrent.

What this viewer is — and isn't

It's a read-only viewer served entirely from the machine you're on. It has:

  • no admin, login, or any way to edit the data
  • no submission or upload form — this protects sources; no legitimate mirror will ever ask you to upload
  • no tracking, no cookies, and no outbound network requests of any kind
This is documentation for research, journalism, and legal accountability. Read the verification guide to confirm any copy is genuine, and the sources for who documented it. The footage is graphic; it is preserved for the public record.
